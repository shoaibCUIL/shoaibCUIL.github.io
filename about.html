About the Computational Linguistics Society

The Computational Linguistics Society (CLS) is a professional and academic organization dedicated to advancing the scientific study of human language through computational methods.

Who We Are

CLS brings together linguists, computer scientists, researchers, educators, and students who share an interest in natural language processing, language technologies, and computational approaches to linguistic analysis. The society serves as a platform for interdisciplinary collaboration and knowledge exchange.

Our Vision

Our vision is to foster a strong and inclusive community that contributes to the development of computational linguistics at local, regional, and international levels, while supporting ethical and responsible language technologies.

Our Mission

  • Promote research and innovation in Computational Linguistics and NLP
  • Support education and capacity building for students and early-career researchers
  • Encourage interdisciplinary collaboration between linguistics and computer science
  • Facilitate academic dialogue through events, workshops, and conferences

Activities & Initiatives

  • Academic seminars and invited talks
  • Hands-on workshops and training programs
  • Research discussion forums and reading groups
  • Collaborative projects and corpus development initiatives

Membership

Membership is open to researchers, students, and professionals with an interest in computational linguistics and related fields. Members gain access to exclusive resources, event registrations, and opportunities for academic collaboration.

Governance

The society is administered by an executive committee consisting of academic and professional members. The committee oversees strategic planning, event organization, and member engagement activities.
